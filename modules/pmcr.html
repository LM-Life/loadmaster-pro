PMCR Rule Used
Missions off station for 16 hours or more receive at least 1 hour of PMCR for each 3 hours off station, up to 96 hours. PMCR begins after all post-flight and post-mission duties are complete. Do not enter pre-departure crew rest until PMCR expires.
Calculation Exact 1:3 ratio, displayed to the nearest minute. This tool supports planning and does not replace SQ/CC direction or governing guidance.
